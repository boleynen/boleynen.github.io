BAUHAUS

100 Years of Bauhaus
with Mart Stam

Mart Stam was a Dutch architect, urban planner, and furniture designer. His career intersects with important moments in the history of 20th-century European architecture, including chair design at the Bauhaus. Stam is most famous for devising a tubular steel cantilever chair in the early 1920s and spawning an entire genre of chair design.
